From a determined student in Cameroon to a tech innovator making global impact, my journey blends technology, education, and social leadership to transform communities.

About Me

I’m Hilux Fokou Ngoumo – a tech innovator, UN youth leader, and social impact advocate. Growing up in Cameroon, I nurtured a passion for change and technology. Today, while pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications at GITAM University in India, I blend my technical expertise with a commitment to community empowerment.

My ventures span developing transformative projects and platforms, speaking at international forums, and authoring research that empowers African youth in the climate crisis. Every step of my journey is driven by the desire to create meaningful impact.
